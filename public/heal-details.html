About The Project

This project focuses on designing a digital solution aimed at supporting individuals experiencing depression. The platform provides free features such as self-assessment tools and daily mood tracking to help users monitor their mental health.

Additionally, it offers paid services, including access to professional support via chat or call, ensuring a comprehensive approach to mental health care while maintaining inclusivity.

Problem Statement
Depression is a prevalent mental health issue, yet many individuals lack access to affordable and convenient resources for monitoring and managing their condition. There is a need for a digital platform that offers both self-help tools and professional support to bridge the gap in mental health care accessibility.
Research Methods
Conducted surveys and interviews with individuals experiencing depression to understand their needs and preferences.
Consulted mental health professionals to design effective self-assessment and mood tracking tools.
Developed user personas to guide the creation of an intuitive and engaging interface.
Findings
Offering both free and paid services ensures inclusivity while catering to those seeking advanced support.
Users highly value tools that enable them to independently monitor their mental health, such as mood tracking and self-assessments.
A user-friendly, empathetic design fosters trust and encourages continued use of the platform.
