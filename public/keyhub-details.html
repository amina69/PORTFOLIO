About The Project

The project focuses on developing a comprehensive educational system to facilitate seamless communication and collaboration between teachers and parents.

The platform includes features such as chat functionality for real-time interaction, access to student academic reports, school fee payment processing with transaction history and receipts, and school-related notifications, among other functionalities.

Problem Statement
Traditional methods of communication between schools and parents are often fragmented and inefficient. There is a growing need for a unified platform that streamlines interactions, enhances transparency in academic reporting, and simplifies administrative tasks such as fee payments and notifications
Research Methods
Analyzed existing school management systems to benchmark features and identify gaps.
Designed and prototyped user interfaces with iterative feedback from stakeholders.
Findings
Parents and teachers benefit significantly from a centralized communication platform, improving engagement and reducing delays in information sharing.
Access to real-time academic reports fosters transparency and enables proactive involvement in students' education.
Timely school notifications enhance awareness and participation in school activities.
